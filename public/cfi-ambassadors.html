CFIs: Empower Your Students, Earn Extra Income

Join the GA Kneeboard Ambassador Program and give your students the tools they need to succeed in the cockpit—while earning rewards for every referral.

1. You Won't Say No to Extra Money

Flight instruction is hard work. We believe you should be rewarded for recommending the tools that help your students fly safely.

For every pilot you refer who subscribes to GA Kneeboard, you'll receive 20% cash or a 30% discount code. It's a simple way to boost your income just by sharing a tool that benefits their training.

2. Students Perform Better When Organized

Cockpit chaos is the enemy of learning. GA Kneeboard helps students master the "Hybrid Cockpit" by generating custom, mission-specific kneeboard sheets (frequencies, checklists, and nav logs).

Instead of fumbling with iPad menus or loose scraps of paper, your students will have clean, organized data right on their leg—allowing them to focus on flying the airplane and listening to your instruction.

Sample VFR Flight Sheet generated by GA Kneeboard

3. Better Debriefs with Paper

Digital flight bags are great for navigation, but terrible for note-taking. With a printed GA Kneeboard sheet, your students have a natural surface to jot down clearances, ATIS, or your in-flight feedback.

During the post-flight debrief, having those handwritten notes makes it easier to review exactly what happened in the air—reinforcing lessons and improving retention.

How to Get Started

Getting your unique ambassador link is easy:

  • Create a free account at GA Kneeboard.
  • Open the Account Details dialog from the main user menu (top right).
  • Locate the Ambassador Link section.
  • Click the copy button and start sharing with your students!
