Aviate Software LLC Launches "GA Kneeboard": Pioneering the Hybrid Cockpit to Reduce Pilot Workload

[Seattle, WA] — January 6, 2026 — Aviate Software LLC today announced the official release of GA Kneeboard, a specialized utility designed to help pilots master the "Hybrid Cockpit." After two years of development and a successful beta program involving over 150 general aviation pilots, GA Kneeboard is now available to the public to help aviators bridge the gap between digital efficiency and analog reliability.

The Rise of the "Hybrid Cockpit"

As cockpits have transitioned to glass panels and iPads, many pilots have found themselves overwhelmed by "screen fatigue." While Electronic Flight Bags (EFBs) are powerful, they can become cumbersome during high-workload phases of flight, such as approach and landing, where digging through app menus poses a safety risk.

GA Kneeboard introduces the concept of the Hybrid Cockpit: a philosophy that uses digital precision to create optimized, physical reference sheets. The software allows pilots to generate custom, high-density data sheets tailored to their specific aircraft and mission, providing "at-a-glance" information that doesn't require a battery, a stylus, or a swipe.

Rooted in Student Pilot Safety

The product was born out of necessity. Created by a team of student pilots who felt the mounting pressure of cockpit workload, GA Kneeboard was designed to make general aviation safer.

"We realized that the iPad, while amazing, had become terrible at providing quick, tactile access to critical data during an emergency or a busy pattern," said [Your Name], Founder of Aviate Software LLC. "We spent two years and hundreds of iterations—grounded in direct feedback from our 150+ beta testers—to ensure this tool actually reduces cognitive load when it matters most."

Key Features of GA Kneeboard

  • Custom Sheet Creation: Build mission-specific kneeboard sheets (frequencies, checklists, and nav logs) that fit standard physical kneeboards.
  • Reduced Cockpit Workload: Simplifies access to information that is often buried three menus deep in traditional EFB apps.
  • Pilot-Vetted Ergonomics: Over 200 usability improvements made during the two-year development cycle based on real-world flight data.
  • Failure-Proof Data Source: Provides a physical "analog" backup to digital systems, ensuring critical data is always visible.

Who It Is For

GA Kneeboard is built for the modern pilot who values safety and organization. It is especially beneficial for student pilots learning to manage radio transitions and cross-country flyers who want to keep their primary EFB screen dedicated to navigation while having their "vital signs" on their leg.

Pricing and Availability

GA Kneeboard is available starting today at https://kneeboard.ga. Prices range from $2.99/month for basic accounts to $4.49/month for private pilots. To celebrate the launch, Aviate Software LLC is offering a lifetime deal for $59.

About Aviate Software LLC

Aviate Software LLC is a pilot-owned software hangar dedicated to building tools that enhance situational awareness and flight safety. By focusing on the intersection of human factors and digital utility, Aviate Software helps pilots fly smarter and safer.
